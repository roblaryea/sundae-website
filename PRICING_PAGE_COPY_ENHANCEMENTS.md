### 2. Sundae Report - Major Value Upgrade ✅

#### Description Enhanced

**Before:**
```
"See where you stand. Free benchmarking against similar restaurants."
```

**After:**
```
"Live dashboards, AI insights, and peer benchmarking—all in one place."
```

**Why:** Positions Report as a comprehensive intelligence platform, not just benchmarking.

#### Features Completely Rewritten

**Before (6 features):**
- Sales, labor, COGS benchmarks
- 100+ KPIs tracked and compared
- Region + segment comparisons
- PDF summary report
- Weekly benchmark refreshes
- Email support

**After (8 features - value-dense):**
1. **Live performance dashboards** built from your daily data
2. **Comparable period views** + target tracking (WoW/MoM/YoY)
3. **AI executive summary**: what changed, why, and next actions
4. **Opportunity & risk flags** vs targets + peers
5. **Anonymous peer benchmarking** (like-for-like)
6. **Shareable charts** + team collaboration
7. **Weekly AI brief** + PDF export
8. Email support

**Key Changes:**
- ✅ Emphasizes **live dashboards** (not just PDF reports)
- ✅ Highlights **AI-powered insights** and executive summaries
- ✅ Shows **team collaboration** capabilities
- ✅ Positions PDF as an export option, not the primary product
- ✅ Makes clear users get **real-time insights**, not just weekly reports

#### CTA Enhanced

**Before:**
```
Button: "Generate My Free Report"
(No microtext)
```

**After:**
```

Microtext: "Custom onboarding based on your data source"
```

**Why:** Less prescriptive, emphasizes personalized onboarding experience.
