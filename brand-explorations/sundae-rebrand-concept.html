The idea
A sundae is the one dessert you can see all the way through. Served tall, in clear glass — every layer at once.
The reframe

We don't sell the ice cream. We own the glass. The promise isn't indulgence — it's total visibility: layering, transparency, completeness. The exact thing an operator wants from their numbers.

Why it's true

Your business is layers — group → brand → region → outlet; revenue → category → daypart → a single item. Sundae lets you see through every one of them, missing nothing. The metaphor and the moat are the same shape.
