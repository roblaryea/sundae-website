# "Decisions, not dashboards" — trio image briefs

The three frames in the homepage **"Decisions, not dashboards"** section
(`SectionCreamRelief` decisions variant) are live operating moments, each with an
overlaid place label (top-left) + a live signal pill with a status dot
(bottom-left). They render at **4:3, `object-cover`**.

## How to replace a frame (zero code changes)

Generate or source the image, then save it over the exact path below. The site
picks it up automatically — no code edit needed.

| Frame | Path | Signal shown | Status dot |
|-------|------|--------------|------------|
| Floor | `public/images/editorial/floor-rush.jpg` | Wait time rising | amber |
| Pass  | `public/images/editorial/pass-fire.jpg`  | Fire time slipping | amber |
| Room  | `public/images/editorial/room-glow.jpg`  | Margin holding | green |

These slots are **independent** of the hero / proof / closer imagery, so changing
them never affects another section. (They are currently seeded with the best-fit
existing photos as placeholders.)

## Shared style (apply to all three)

Premium, cinematic, *real and operational* — not generic stock. Warm low-light
restaurant atmosphere, editorial photography, shallow depth of field, natural
motion. **Aspect ratio 4:3 (landscape).** Leave a **darker, uncluttered area in
the top-left and bottom-left corners** for a small overlay label + a live-signal
pill (so keep the subject and highlights off the far left edge).

**Avoid:** visible brand logos or signage, any text in the image, posed/cheesy
stock smiles, empty/sterile rooms, bright flat studio lighting, calm
no-pressure plating close-ups, cartoon/illustration, and AI-looking hands or
faces (favour crops where hands are partial/in-motion or faces are turned/soft).

## Frame 1 — FLOOR · "Wait time rising"

> A server or floor team member moving through a busy dining room during service —
> carrying plates, checking a table, or responding to guests. Service in motion
> with a slight sense of urgency; a real restaurant floor with occupied tables
> softly out of focus behind. Warm low light, shallow depth of field, natural
> motion blur acceptable. 4:3. Darker space in the bottom-left and top-left for an
> overlay. No posed smiles, no empty tables, no flat stock lighting, no text or
> logos.

## Frame 2 — PASS · "Fire time slipping"

> A kitchen pass / line during active service: chefs plating under heat lamps,
> food on the pass, pans on flame, an expo area with a ticket rail, hands finishing
> dishes at speed. It should feel hot, time-sensitive and operational — pressure,
> not calm. Steam/heat haze, warm tungsten + flame light, shallow depth of field.
> 4:3. Darker space in the bottom-left and top-left for an overlay. No clean studio
> food shots, no calm low-pressure plating close-ups, no text or logos, no
> AI-looking hands.

## Frame 3 — ROOM · "Margin holding"

> A warm dining room *in service* — guests at tables, wine glasses, soft staff
> movement, a visible flow of covers. It should suggest revenue and atmosphere in
> motion (a premium full house at golden hour / candlelight), not an empty room.
> Warm low light, shallow depth of field, gentle motion in the background. 4:3.
> Darker space in the bottom-left and top-left for an overlay. No empty rooms
> (unless the composition clearly reads as reserved/premium quiet), no flat stock
> lighting, no text or logos.
